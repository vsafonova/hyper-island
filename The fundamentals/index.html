Slapstick humor

It is a physical kind of humor.
This means someone is using exaggerated or wild body movements.

Usually, there are gags, pranks, and practical jokes involved but it always has to do with the body, or comedy around the body behaving oddly. Oftentimes people are getting hurt, maybe they slip and fall, maybe something is falling on them. It can be painful to watch at times and yet it brings out the laughter in us humans.
